Hello! My name is Sai Roop Somaraju, and I’m currently working as a Wi-Fi Firmware Engineer at Apple Inc., I graduated with a Master’s degree in Computer Engineering from Virginia Tech'24, where my research focused on enhancing the eBPF subsystem within the Linux kernel to improve safety and robustness, under the guidance of Dr. Dan Williams at the ROSA Lab.

When I'm not engaged in programming, I enjoy backpacking in the woods, listening to music, and cooking.
